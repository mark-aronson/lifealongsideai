## Reflecting on Humanity 

### Purpose
Before we start talking about information processing, we'll take a moment of pause to reflect on who we are and what it means to be human. 

### Activity 
Take 15 minutes to walk with no distractions. Reflect on the times in your life (a specific memory or experience or a general activity or place) where you felt the most human and/or closest to your humanity. 

We'll then share out some of our reflections with the class. 

Then take another walk and reflect on when in your life you felt the least human, or furthest from your humanity. 

We'll again share out reflections, contrasting the two, and create our prior for the class of what it means to be human. 

### Deliverable 
Upload a picture of your reflection to Brightspace. 

### Grading
I am looking for you to answer the two prompts and reasons why.
