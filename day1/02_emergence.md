# Life Alongside AI

## Emergence Activity 

### Purpose
A key theme of this class is the idea of Emergence - how different properties arise from the sum of their parts. We will use this activity to understand what emergence means and identify an emergent property in a system of our choosing. 

### Assignment
We'll begin with [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=16W7c0mb-rE) that explores the idea of emergence. 

### Activity 
Pick a complex system, think about its “stupid parts” and the rules that lead to emergent properties. 
- possible examples: social media algorithms, dating apps, global supply chains 

### Deliverable 
Submit a paragraph describing your system of choice, the "stupid parts," and the rules that lead to emergent properties. Upload this to Brightspace.
