# Life Alongside AI

## Biological Intelligence Project

### Purpose
In day 1, we covered the idea of emergence - how new properties emerge from parts and rules, and some examples of biological systems and behaviors emerge from simple molecular rules. In this project, you will apply this lens to a biological system of your choosing. 

### Assignment 
Pick a biological system (can be as simple as a tissue or organ or an entire biosphere) and break it down to its simple parts and the rules that govern it. Explain how these rules lead to emergent properties of the system. 

### Deliverable 
In a format of your choosing (written report, a few slides, etc.), describe the complex system, its emergent behavior, its parts, and the rules that describe how those parts following the rules lead to the emergent behavior.

### Grading 
Grades will be assigned on the articulation of a complex biological system, an emergent property, the parts and rules and how they create that emergent properties.
