# Life Alongside AI

## Is AI Making Us Lazy?

### Purpose
Now that we have covered the concepts in biological information processing, AI, and human-machine relational frameworks, it's time to start putting this all together. We'll start with a reflection activity to start thinking about how ought we, as beings with biological intelligence, to live in a world of increasingly powerful AI?

### Activity 
Read Cal Newport's piece ["Is AI Making Us Lazy?"](https://calnewport.com/does-ai-make-us-lazy/) and respond in a jouranl entry. 

### Deliverable 
An uploaded picture of your journal entry in Brightspace. 

### Grading 
I am looking for whether you agree or disagree with Newport's thesis and backing up your position with your personal experience and ideas.
