# Life Alongside AI

## Final Project

### Purpose
Now it's time to put all the pieces together and answer the question we have been circling all week: how ought we, as beings with biological intelligence, to live in a world of increasingly powerful AI? 

### Activity 
The format of the project is open-ended: it can be an essay, a presentation, a spoken word poem, a piece of visual art, a code repository, a short play, a videogame... 

The guidelines are: 
1. Must speak to the human experience in a world of AI 
2. Must pull on some other theme from the class (emergence, human machine relationships)

### Schedule 
The project will take the day following this schedule: 
1. Project introduction and questions 
2. Initial brainstorm (1 hour)
3. Project pitches (each present for 3 minutes)
4. Work block (~4 hours)
5. Presentations with pizza 

### Grading 
I will be looking for your ability to apply the concepts of the course and to answer the question of how we ought to live, and looking for creativity in the presentation of your answer.
