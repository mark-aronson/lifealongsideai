# Life Alongside AI

## Machine Relatinos 

### Purpose
Today's focus is on frameworks of understanding human relationships to machines. How the history of technological development led to different frameworks and the aspects of those different frameworks. After working to understand them, we will see if any offer useful lenses on our relationship with AI today. 

### Activity 
For each question, we'll take five minutes of quiet journaling before doing a classwide shareout. 

#### Finding a metaphor 
Describe relationship with device as a metaphor and explain why you feel this is an apt metaphor. 

#### Feature to behavior mapping 
Pick a feature of a piece of technology you use (e.g. infinite scroll on Instagram, TikTok's algorithm). Describe how the design of that feature drives your behavior around that piece of technology. Consider the incentives (economic, cultural, philosophical) that underlie the creation and design of that feature. 

#### Explore an alternate history
How might your relationship with this machine be different if it was designed by different people for different purposes?
