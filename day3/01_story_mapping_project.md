# Life Alongside AI

## Story Mapping Project 

### Purpose
Now that we have covered the history of human-machine relational frameworks, we will test our understanding by applying them to a piece of fiction. 

### Activity 
Pick a piece of narrative fiction. Sci-fi seems like a natural genre, but perhaps there are other genres that work in interesting ways. Write an analysis of how this piece of fiction applies or interrogates a human-machine relational framework (can use one of the ones we discussed, but also feel free to find ones we did not cover). Consider questions like: 
- Does the story interrogate one framework, or use characters or factions to compare two frameworks? 
- Does the story take a stand on a "correct" framework or way of relating to machines? 

### Deliverable 
Create a 5 slide presentation on your analysis. Present to the class (3-5min) and upload your slides to Brightspace. 

### Grading Expectations 
I will be grading both your analysis (7 points) and your presentation (3 points). I am looking for the ability to persuasively apply one or more of the frameworks.
