# Life Alongside AI

## Data Reduction Activity 

### Purpose
In this activity, we are going to explore the capturing of reality by data and reflect on the experience of being captured by data. 

### Know Yourself 
Let's begin by thinking about what makes you, you. Take some time to journal the things that define you as an individual person. 

### Quantify Yourself 
Look back at your list of personal qualities and think how you would capture those things in data, in a way something can be recorded. 

### Reflect
What aspects of you are captured by your quantified metrics? What aspects are not? How does data create an imperfect picture of our reality?
