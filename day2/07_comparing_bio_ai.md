# Life Alongside AI

## Comparing Biology and AI

### Purpose
Now that we dove into the details of biological information processing and AI, let's start with a comparison between the two systems. 

### Activity 
First on your own, create a Venn Diagram of components, themes, or principles of biological and digital information processing. Look at your list and reflect - what surprises you? Were any similarities borderline? Can you make an argument either way? 

We will then have a group discussion about our reflections and we'll see if we have consensus or disagreement about how we classified different aspects of biological and artifical information processing. 

Lastly, after our discussion, take some time to synthesize and craft a statement about what it means to be on the biological side of this dyad: what is distinct? What is shared? 

### Deliverable 
Upload your Venn Diagram and your post-discussion reflection to Brightspace. 

### Grading 
I am looking for articulation of at least one thing unique to biology, one unique to AI, and one shared and why; participation in the group discussion; and a thoughtful synthesis of these parts in your final reflection.
